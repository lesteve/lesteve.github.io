Mission

Software Carpentry's mission is to help scientists and engineers get more research done in less time and with less pain by teaching them basic lab skills for scientific computing.

Target Audience

  • Scientists without prior knowledge
  • My 2c: mostly beneficial to scientists who have at least a hint they have problems that programming could solve better
  • My 2.5c: even better if you have previous exposure to a programming language
Truer than life examples at https://software-carpentry.org/audience

Typical SWC workshop

  • shell: 1/2 day
  • git + github: 1/2 day
  • Python: 1 day

Our teaching method

Basic principles

  • Live coding
  • Lessons materials on github with a Creative Commons licence
  • Frequent quizzes and hands-on exercises to keep audience interested/awake
  • Feed-back: red/green sticky notes, Etherpad during and after each session, pre- and post-workshop survey
